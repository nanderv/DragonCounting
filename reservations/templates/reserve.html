Fill in your name and when you want to visit. Each timeslot has a limited capacity; if it's full, please select another one. For registering, the following rules apply:

  • The timeslots open two days before a day
  • Timeslots close on the day before, at 16:00
  • Timeslots stay open if a timeslot isn't full yet, but isn't empty either.
The last rule is there, because we don't want our openers to have to keep the room open if nobody wants to visit.
Observe that on the day itself, it's possible to register for all timeslots on the day, even ones that already took place.

Privacy:

This data will be visible to people who can open the room, during the day for which the registration was made.
The fact that somebody registered a visit is visible to all openers from the moment of the registration, until the registration is removed from the system.
The data submitted here will be stored for less than forty-eight hours after the end of the timeslot for which the registration was made.
Besides this, our web committee has access to the data, in case of technical issues. The web committee also cannot access the data beyond forty-eight hours after the end of the timeslot. {% endblock %}
